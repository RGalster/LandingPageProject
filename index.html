Who is Mr Coach? For this project I've come up with a fictional client named Mr Coach, a "regular Joe" with a 9 to 5 who's starting a side-hustle coaching adult volleyball players evenings and weekends. With a growing need to better organize training sessions as well as provide more convenience to players and coaches alike, Mr Coach sought out a number of technical solutions in what has become a custom Web App called My Volleyball Coach. Coaches and other staff can set up training sessions and scrimmage matches, players can easily find activities that fit their goals, and all the pertinent details can be found in one place. Each session will stay live on the site until the posted end time so that only upcoming events will be visible. Both upcoming and past sessions make use of the cloud to store data so that Mr Coach can easily sort through all his records.
